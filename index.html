01 — About

Engineer by training, communicator by instinct.

I'm Viraj, a Computer Engineering diploma graduate now reading for a bachelor's in Computer Science. My work sits at the crossing of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and full-stack web development — I like problems that are equal parts engineering and judgment.

Off the keyboard, I'm a trained public speaker and debater. I've stood on competitive stages arguing both sides of hard questions, and I bring that same habit of building the counter-argument into every piece of software I ship.

I interned at Interface IT Solution, where I taught coding to students and ran the studio's social presence — a rare mix of classroom and client-facing work that shaped how I explain technical ideas simply. That teaching instinct is now the seed of SkoolOfAI, an educational initiative I'm growing to make AI genuinely learnable.
